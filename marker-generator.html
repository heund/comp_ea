Exhibition AR Marker Generator

Generate 5 cohesive AR markers for your steel manufacturing exhibition

How to Use:

  1. Select a theme that matches your exhibition aesthetic
  2. Click "Generate All Markers" to create the 5-marker series
  3. Download PNG files for each marker individually
  4. Convert to .patt files using AR.js Pattern Generator: AR.js Generator
  5. Update your artwork files to use the new marker patterns
